Investment Approach

Strategic technology investments designed to scale across all Auburn Athletics venues

Phased Rollout

Start with the highest-impact venues — Jordan-Hare and Neville Arena — then expand proven technology templates across remaining facilities.

Scalable Design

Each focus area is built as a reusable technology package. What works for 88,000 seats scales down to 1,500 — same platform, same management.

Quick Wins First

Many improvements — IPTV distribution, digital signage, mobile wayfinding — can be deployed in weeks, not months. Visible impact on day one.

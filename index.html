People Tracking & Counting

Real-time crowd intelligence across every Auburn venue — smarter operations, better fan experience

Operations Intelligence

Know Your Crowd in Real Time

From gate entry to concession lines, restroom waits to concourse flow — AI-powered sensors and analytics give Auburn operations the data to make faster decisions, reduce bottlenecks, and improve the fan experience at every touchpoint.

30% Shorter Wait Times
25% More F&B Revenue
40% Faster Response
99% Count Accuracy
🚪

Gate & Entrance Counting

Overhead 3D stereo-vision sensors at every gate provide exact entry counts, detect tailgating, and feed real-time gate density to the operations dashboard and fan mobile app for optimized entry flow.

🏃

Concourse Flow Monitoring

AI-powered cameras analyze crowd density, flow direction, and dwell time across concourses. Operations see bottlenecks forming before they become problems. Fans get green/yellow/red indicators via the mobile app.

🍔

Concession Wait Times

Overhead cameras with AI analyze queue length and service rate at every stand. Wait times are displayed on digital signage and pushed to the fan app — redirecting fans to shorter lines and increasing per-capita spending.

🚻

Restroom Occupancy

Throughput sensors, stall occupancy detection, and smart consumable monitoring (soap, paper, supplies). Red/yellow/green indicators on hallway digital signs direct fans to the nearest available facility.

⭐

Suite & Premium Occupancy

Radar-based sensors (no cameras, zero PII) in suites and premium clubs provide anonymous occupancy counts. Validates capacity, supports dynamic premium pricing, and ensures VIP experience quality.

🅿️

Parking & Lot Monitoring

Camera-based virtual zoning counts vehicles by area without physical infrastructure changes. Real-time lot availability on LED signs and the mobile app guides fans to open spots and reduces pre-game congestion.

Recommended Technology Partners

WaitTime

Top Pick — Crowd Intelligence

Patented AI platform that processes overhead camera feeds at 24fps to generate real-time crowd density overlays, queue analysis, and wait time estimates across entire venue concourses.

  • Real-time concourse heat maps & flow visualization
  • Concession & restroom wait time estimation
  • Fan-facing mobile app integration (green/yellow/red)
  • Operations dashboard with historical analytics
  • Works with existing Axis camera infrastructure
Deployed: Ohio State, Alabama, LA Dodgers, Man City

Axis P8815-2 3D Counter

Top Pick — Gate Counting

Ceiling-mounted 3D stereo-vision sensor combining hardware and software in one device. Uses dual sensors to generate depth maps, distinguishing people from objects with bi-directional counting.

  • 99%+ accuracy with tailgating detection
  • PoE powered, no license required
  • Native integration with Genetec Security Center
  • Link two units for wide entrance coverage
  • Industry standard across major venues
Deployed: Ohio State (100+ cameras), NFL & MLB venues

TRAX SmartRestroom

Top Pick — Restrooms

Purpose-built venue restroom intelligence platform integrating 45+ sensor types — throughput counters, stall occupancy, smart dispensers, feedback kiosks, and staff tracking beacons.

  • Real-time occupancy & supply level monitoring
  • Clean+Inspect staff task management
  • Digital signage integration for wait indicators
  • 80% operational efficiency improvement
  • Time-stamped proof-of-service compliance
Deployed: Progressive Field (56 facilities monitored)

Density (Radar-Based)

Top Pick — Premium Spaces

Radar-based (no cameras, zero PII) anonymous people counting. The "Waffle" sensor self-installs in suites; the "Open Area" sensor covers large premium clubs with 2x range of camera alternatives.

  • Zero visual surveillance — complete privacy
  • Self-installable across dozens of suites
  • Real-time cloud dashboard with open API
  • 10 frames/second low-latency data
  • Ideal for suites, clubs, and lounges
Deployed: Fortune 500 campuses, airport lounges

Wicket Express Entry

Recommended — Facial Auth

Opt-in facial authentication for hands-free express gate entry. Fans upload a selfie, link their ticket, and walk through QuickLane lanes — no phone needed. All biometric data encrypted, never shared.

  • 5M+ total scans and growing
  • Biometric-certain gate counting (no tailgating)
  • Traditional ticket scan always available
  • Expanding into college athletics market
  • Encrypted biometric data, never sold
Deployed: University of Florida, Tampa Bay Bucs

Cisco Spaces + Meraki Wi-Fi

Recommended — Heat Maps

Turns existing Cisco Meraki Wi-Fi access points into location sensors. Generates venue-wide heat maps from device detection — crowd density, dwell time, path analysis, all without additional hardware.

  • Zero additional hardware if Meraki is deployed
  • Venue-wide coverage from existing infrastructure
  • Playback function to replay event crowd flow
  • Zone-level occupancy & dwell analytics
